About

About Us

At SRI THAMBURAN TAILORING, we specialize in delivering high-quality uniform tailoring services that seamlessly blend traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. Serving schools, corporations, healthcare providers, and various institutions across Thrissur and Kerala, we ensure each uniform reflects professionalism, comfort, and durability.

Services

Our Services

Custom Uniform Tailoring

We provide tailored uniforms for a diverse range of sectors:

• Educational Institutions:
School uniforms designed for comfort and durability.
• Corporate Entities:
Professional attire that aligns with your brand identity.
• Healthcare Providers:
Functional and comfortable uniforms for medical staff.
• Hospitality Industry:
Elegant uniforms for hotels, restaurants, and resorts.
• Public Services:
Uniforms for law enforcement, fire services, and other public sectors.

Alteration Services

Our skilled tailors offer precise alterations to ensure a perfect fit:

• Hemming of trousers and skirts.
• Adjustments to sleeves, waistlines, and lengths.
• Resizing and refitting services.

Embroidery and Branding

Enhance your uniforms with our embroidery services:

• Custom logos and emblems.
• Name tags and badges.
• Department-specific insignias.

Fabric Selection and Consultation

We assist you in selecting the right fabric that balances comfort, durability, and aesthetics:

• Breathable cotton blends for tropical climates.
• Easy-care fabrics for daily wear.
• Premium materials for executive uniforms.
